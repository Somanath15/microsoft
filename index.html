Microsoft Overview

Microsoft is a technology company that produces software, electronics, and personal computers. The most popular software products include the Windows line of operating systems, the Microsoft Office suite, and the Internet Explorer and Edge web browsers.

Founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, Microsoft is one of the largest and most influential tech companies in the world.

Microsoft is also known for its cloud computing platform, Azure, and its hardware products like the Xbox gaming consoles and Surface tablets.
